# Isn't that crazy?

This energiser allows you to flex your imagination. Here's how it works:

1. You'll be going around in a circle, clockwise.
2. The person who's birthday is coming up next starts by saying "Isn't that crazy?".
3. The next person continues by adding 3 words to the story like "that Luigi has".
4. Go round and round until the story is complete!
