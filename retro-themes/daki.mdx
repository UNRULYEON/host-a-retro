# DAKI - Drop, Add, Keep, Improve

## Host

Write down 4 stickies and put them on the board dividing it into 4 quadrants:

- Drop
- Add
- Keep
- Remove

## Everyone

1. Write down stickies that fit into one of the 4 quadrants.
2. The person living the furthest from the office goes first.
3. Place each sticky in the appropriate area and give a short explanation.
4. Appoint the next person to go.
5. Group the stickies by theme.
6. Vote!
