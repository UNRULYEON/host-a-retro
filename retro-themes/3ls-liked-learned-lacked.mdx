# The 3 Ls: Liked, Learned, Lacked

## Host

Write down 3 stickies and place them on the board:

- Liked
- Learned
- Lacked

## Everyone

1. Write down stickies that fit in each area:

- **Liked** - things you really liked.
- **Learned** - things you have learned.
- **Lacked** - things you wish had happened.

2. Whoever is the youngest goes first. Place each sticky in the appropriate area and give a short explanation.
3. Appoint the next person to go.
4. Group the stickies by theme.
5. Vote!
